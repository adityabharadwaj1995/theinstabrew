ABOUT US

Experience tea, the easy and flavorful way!

FIRST OF IT'S KIND

A unique concept of packing the fresh organic green tea leaves inside the cup with an infusion layer. All you have to do is just pour hot water to the required level and leave it for couple of minutes for the tea to infuse in the hot water. The infusion layer acts as a filter and seals the tea leaves at the bottom of the cup, and hence every sip will be completely safe cum healthy and is hassle free while drinking. Our tea cups are able to address the said issues by supplying quality and hygienically manufactured beverages at an affordable prices offering in the most convenient experiential way. Our products are available in various flavour variant and is travel and workplace friendly.
