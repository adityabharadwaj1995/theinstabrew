At The Insta Brew, we bring a fresh and innovative approach to tea drinking with our unique tea cups. Each cup is packed with fresh organic green tea leaves, sealed by an infusion layer that allows you to simply add hot water and enjoy. The infusion layer ensures a smooth, safe, and healthy tea experience by keeping the leaves at the bottom, offering a hassle-free, enjoyable sip every time. Our tea cups are hygienically crafted, affordable, and available in various flavors, making them perfect for travel and workplace convenience. Experience tea, the easy and flavorful way!




FIRST OF IT'S KIND

CUP MATERIAL
  • Insta brew Cups made up of 270 GSM revolutionary food grade paper with no wax coating And 100% biodegradable, which serves exceptionally amazing cup of freshness in each sip.
TEA QUALITY
  • We are using fresh organic tea leaves procured via fair trade from the best tea estates in India which are ground using regulated conditions and packaged to perfection.
INFUSION LAYER
  • High quality designed temperature Techno Layer, which allows only hot water to pass and mix with the tea at regulated rate to ensure that your tea is at its best when you take a sip.
